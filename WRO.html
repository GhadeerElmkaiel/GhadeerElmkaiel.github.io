May, 2013 - September, 2017

WRO Robotics competition
trainer

I started working at SCS (Syrian Computer Society) as a team trainer for the WRO (World Robot Olympiad) competition in May 2013. I was one of the first WRO trainers in Syria.
My teams participated in the national competition in 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, and 2017. Many of my teams achieved first, or second place over multiple categories, and qualified for the world finals multiple times.
In 2016, my team participated in the WRO world finals in India.
Other than training, I taught multiple robotics-oriented courses as programming for Arduino, in which, I taught my students the elementary principles of programming. I also taught different programming, mechanisms, and electronics courses.
My students were aged between 7 and 18 years old.
